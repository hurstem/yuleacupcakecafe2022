Welcome To

Yulea's Cupcake Cafe

We offer the finest baked goods in Chicago. We offer sandwiches, coffee, and cupcakes for any and all events. You can choose from a list of pre-planned cupcakes flavors or you can create your own custom cupcake flavors and topping for your special event. With our expert bakers, we can make almost any cupcake dream come true. We also include drinks and sandwiches for up to 300 people!

Whether your event be a wedding, a birthday, or even an office meeting; we hope that you choose Yulea’s Cupcakes to be the one that makes it the best day ever.
