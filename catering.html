Drinks

Drinks are made by the gallon, we measure how much is needed by the gallon, examples are below. Each event by default will have regular (medium roast) and decaf coffee (medium roast) as well as hot water for teas (that Yulea's Cupcake Cafe will provide) unless a Speciality Drink (listed below) is selected.
