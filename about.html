Who is Yulea?

Yulea Nilsson is a Swedish-American born on November 23rd, 1955. She started working in her family’s deli at first, to help clean up counters and wash the windows. She started baking with her grandmother at 11; creating Prinsesstårta (princess cake), Äppelkaka (apple cake), Våfflor (Swedish waffles), and other desserts from Sweden.

However, her true passion was cupcakes.

Yulea went to the Kendall College at National Louis University, a private college in Chicago, Illinois specializing in culinary arts and hospitality management in 1973. Once she got her degree in 1977, she moved back in with her grandmother and started Yulea’s Cupcakes in her kitchen, experimenting and creating cupcakes and other pastries to sell to the families that lived around her street. As her popularity grew, she bought a store front on 225 E Chicago Ave, Chicago in 1982 and has been there ever since.

Yulea has always stood for creativity, craftsmanship, hard-work, and most importantly, food. Her mission for her cafe was to spread love and acceptance through her cupcakes. With her and her baker’s experimentation; new cupcakes and new ideas are created everyday for everyone to enjoy.

Our Services

Local Catering

We offer the finest baked goods in Chicago. We offer sandwiches, coffee, and cupcakes for any and all events. You can choose from a list of pre-planned cupcakes flavors or you can create your own custom cupcake flavors and topping for your special event. With our expert bakers, we can make almost any cupcake dream come true. We also include drinks and sandwiches for up to
300 people!

Cafe and Bakery

Located in Yulea's home of Chicago, we have a cafe and bakery for all to enjoy. From cupcakes to sandwiches, milkshakes, smoothies, coffee, and iced drinks; we have everything you could need at our cafe. Along with our food products, we also have merchandise and ground coffee to purchase for your enjoyment!
